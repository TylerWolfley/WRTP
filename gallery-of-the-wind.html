Gallery of the Wind

A museum space inside the trading post

The Gallery of the Wind gives Wind River Trading Post a different kind of depth. Alongside the store, the museum space holds artifacts and artwork connected to the Eastern Shoshone and Northern Arapaho peoples.

Artifacts and artwork

The museum space is part of what makes the stop feel different. It adds context, quiet, and weight, giving the building more than one purpose without turning it into a staged attraction.

Under the same roof

Store and museum sit together rather than competing with each other. That pairing is part of the identity of the place: one side rooted in objects people can take with them, the other rooted in objects meant to be looked at more slowly.

The Gallery of the Wind is part of Wind River Trading Post in Fort Washakie on Highway 287.
