Inside the Store

The Trading Post

Wind River Trading Post is built around the feel of a real trading post rather than a catalog. Jewelry, beadwork, clothing, art, and practical goods sit together in one space, with materials, colors, and objects doing more of the work than signage ever could.

01

Jewelry & Beadwork

The jewelry room has a quieter kind of draw. Cases of silver, beadwork, and small objects sit under the lights without feeling over-arranged, so the space invites a slower look instead of a quick pass.

02

Clothing & Textiles

Clothing and textiles add warmth and weight to the store. Rather than feeling like a fashion rack, these pieces help define the room through pattern, material, and color.

03

Art & Regional Goods

Art, objects, and regional goods keep the store from feeling narrow or over-categorized. The mix is part of the character of the place, and the inventory can shift over time instead of reading like a fixed product lineup.
